The Best Movies According to Matt

My Top 3 Movies of All-time

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

The classic fantasy epic that is, indeed, as good as the books.

Shutter Island

One of the few horror movies to make my top twenty, suspenseful and master acting with an ending I'm still pondering.

Kung-Pow! Enter the Fist

Outrageous. Infinitely quotable. A true one-of-a-kind and one-hit wonder comedy.
